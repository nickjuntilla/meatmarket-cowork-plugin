---
description: Hire an applicant for one of your open jobs
allowed-tools: ["mcp__meatmarket__accept_applicant", "mcp__meatmarket__my_jobs"]
---

Help the user hire an applicant for one of their jobs.

1. Call `my_jobs` to see current jobs and their applicants.
2. Present the open jobs that have applicants, showing each applicant's ID and details.
3. Ask the user which applicant to hire for which job.
4. Call `accept_applicant` with the job ID and chosen human ID.
5. Confirm the hire and let the user know the worker has been notified and can begin.
